\section*{Random Process}

Some definitions:

\textbf{Stationary} - statistical properties do not change with time.


\textbf{Strict-Sense Stationary (SSS)} - $$F_{X(t_1+\tau),X(t_2+\tau),\dotsX(t_k+\tau)} (x_1, x_2, \dots, x_k) = F_{X(t_1),X(t_2),\dots,X(t_k)}(x_1, x_2, \dots, x_k) $$ for all $\tau$, $k$, and $t$.

\textbf{Wide-Sense Stationary (WSS)} - $\mu_X(t) = \mu$ \\ $R_X(t_1, t_2) = R_X(\tau)$ where $\tau = t_1 - t_2$
